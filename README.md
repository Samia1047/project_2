Expense Reimbursement System (ERS) - Project2
In this document, all requirements and required technologies pertaining the full stack group project of your training will be presented.
A All requirements are mandatory. The optional requirement of uploading receipt image is also to be completed. 
There is a shift in technologies as well. The Expense Reimbursement System will manage the process of reimbursing employees for expenses incurred while on company time.
All employees in the company can login and submit requests for reimbursement and view their past tickets and pending requests. 
Finance managers can log in and view all reimbursement requests and history for all employees in the company. 
Finance managers are authorized to approve and deny requests for expense reimbursement.
